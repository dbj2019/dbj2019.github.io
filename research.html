circadian clocks regulates the daily physiology of an organism according to the time of the day. Transcription and Translational Feedback Loops (TTFLs) are believed to be the underlined molecular mechanism responsible for this daily regulation of the physiology. However, recent studies have shown that the clock controls the physiology beyond TTFLs at the post-transcriptioan or translational levels. In my PhD project, using the single cell algae I investigate regulation of the clock at the posttranscriptioanl and translational level
